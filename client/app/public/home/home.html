Logos

You are starting on the path to improve yourself mentally and physically. The Logos pathway is where it all starts. In this pathway your fundamental movements will be solidified in a safe an effective manner in order to increase your success in the program.

Pathos

You have mastered the basics and have formed a new commitment to excellence. By now you should have defined your purpose in coming to the gym and plan on taking the next step in proving yourself.

Ethos

Ethos is an appeal to ethics, and it is a means of convincing someone of their character or credibility. In the ethos path you will develop skills that will better yourself as a person. These excericses will strengthen yourself physically and mentally.

How it Works

1
Sign Up

Signing up for the program is the fastest way to get started. Users will pick their CrossFit gym during the registration process. After signing up you will immediately be a member of your gym's Logos Academy.

2
Follow Curriculum

Our expert-developed curriculum sets attainable goals for your to progress through the Logos Academy. Physical movements are accompanied by instructional tips and videos ensuring a clear understanding of the requirements.

3
Achieve Your Goals

Setting and achieving your goals are a fundamental part of CrossFit. This is how we measure our growth. The Logos Academy allows you to set and maintain your goals through our easy-to-use interface.
